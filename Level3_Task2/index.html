Brief Introduction:

At Cognifyz Technologies, we offer dynamic web developer internship opportunities designed to provide hands-on experience and mentorship in the field of web development. As an intern, you'll have the chance to work on real projects, collaborate with experienced professionals, and enhance your skills in front-end and back-end development. With a flexible schedule and a focus on growth and networking, our internship program sets you on the path to success in the exciting world of web development.

The internship duration is 1 months.

Qualifications:
  • Proficiency in programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • A strong portfolio showcasing projects or coursework related to web development. This could include websites, web applications, or other relevant projects.
  • Familiarity with responsive design principles
  • Experience with version control systems (e.g., Git)
  • While these are general qualifications, we also consider candidates with diverse backgrounds and experiences. We value enthusiasm, dedication, and a willingness to grow as a developer.
